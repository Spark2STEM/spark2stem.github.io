Our Mission

What is Spark2STEM?

We're on a mission to spark curiosity, creativity, and a love for STEM through stories that inspire children to dream, discover, and innovate. By creating engaging books, hands-on workshops, and educational resources, we empower young minds to explore the endless possibilities of STEM!

Curriculum

Our collection of STEM-themed children's books introduces young readers to exciting concepts in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through engaging stories and relatable characters. Each book is designed to spark curiosity, inspire creativity, and help children discover the possibilities of STEM in a fun and accessible way.

How to Launch a Chapter

Bringing Spark2STEM to your school is a direct way to build leadership experience while offering vital science and engineering mentorship to young students in your city. We provide a structured pipeline to scale operations effectively from day one.

1

Submit Your Application

Fill out the application form with basic information about your school and the students who will help lead the chapter.

2

Interview

Meet with our team to talk about your goals, plans for your chapter, and how we can support you.

3

Launch Support

Once you're approved, we'll provide resources, training materials, and step-by-step guidance to help you start and run your chapter successfully.

Start Your Chapter

We supply new student founders with complete lesson templates, verified volunteer credit logging validation, direct administrative approval guides, and access to our global workshop system repository.
